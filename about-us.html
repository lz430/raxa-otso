About Us

OTSO Energy Solutions is an EPC company offering a diversified portfolio of Modularized Equipment for the Oil & Gas sector. Our expertise is centrally focused on meeting the critical needs of our Upstream, Midstream and Power Generation Customers. OTSO is an independent fit-for-purpose solutions provider with extensive experience in the Oil & Gas and Power Generation market segments.

Our people-oriented culture translates into innovative, energy efficient and environmental friendly projects. OTSO’s experienced, hands-on management team offers clients peace of mind throughout their project. Our experienced project management, planning and engineering team offers clients an additional resource to utilize while meeting the ever increasing demands of their industries.

We appreciate your interest in OTSO Energy Solutions. If you have questions or need assistance, please give us a call @ 111-111-1111 or contact us via email @ info@otsoenergy.com.
