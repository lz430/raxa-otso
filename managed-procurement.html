Managed Procurement

OTSO Energy Solutions Managed Procurement Service (MPS) gives access to procurement expertise on demand, enabling our customers to deliver value to the business and realize bottom line improvement. MPS is unique in that it is applicable not just to the commonly outsourced indirect spend categories, but also to direct, core and project spend.

OTSO Energy Solutions Managed Procurement Service (MPS) supports your procurement operations through technology deployment, interim resource augmentation and procurement transformation.

MPS covers a mix of services appropriate to your requirements, including Sourcing and eSourcing, Supplier Management, Category Management, and Technology Implementation to deliver benefits through people, technology and expertise.
